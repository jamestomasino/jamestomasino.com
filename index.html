James Tomasino

Technology Manager at GSW, web developer gypsy, US Navy veteran, Eagle Scout, former monk; James brings a broad variety of experience to his work. He holds a Masters in Media Arts and Science from Indiana University and has worked in Interactive Media since 2005 on clients ranging from Verizon to Sesame Street, the Department of Environmental Conservation to the WWF, from 20th Century Fox to the Home Depot, and many more in between. You can find him all over the web.

CoderBits

Those in the web industry contribute and learn in a lot of disconnected places. We share knowledge on StackOverflow and pick up new skills on CodeAcademy. We share our open source projects on BitBucket and read the latest trends on HackerNews. With this distribution of interactivity it's difficult to gather together a true sense of an individual.

Coderbits is a site that tries to solve the problem by aggregating all of that big data to create visualizations and award badges. Below are a few charts that show you what James is all about by letting his actions online tell the story.
